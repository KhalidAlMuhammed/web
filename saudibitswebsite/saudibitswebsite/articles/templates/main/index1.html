Our Vision

Our vision is to make computer science and programming accessible and available to everyone in Saudi Arabia regardless of language. Because technical skills are important in our time, we have dedicated ourselves to helping develop these skills to anyone with a passion to learn.



Our Mission

Our mission is to build a community and environment based on collaboration. We provide guidance, courses, competitions, and more to expand programming and computer science skills at all levels in both English and Arabic.
